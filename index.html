Principles

Some principles that have guided my career.

  • Customer value over velocity. Shipping fast feels good. Shipping something that works feels better.
  • Design systems over one-off solutions. A good pattern used everywhere beats a perfect solution used once.
  • Standards over advocacy. Baking quality in beats fighting for it later.
  • Build to find clarity. When no one knows the answer, making something usually finds it.

Current working process

I reached this process through years of experimenting with different AI tools.

  1. Data gathering, insights, explorations, competitive/non-competitive/adjacent
  2. Consult LLM to build domain knowledge, fill gaps, ask for POVs (Example)
  3. Generate a wireframe (ascii lofi or visually rich depending on the requirements)
  4. Map flows/broader system interactions in drawing tool (Figma)
  5. Share with team on Loom for feedback
  6. Hand over to designer on team / created detailed mocks in Figma using Design System
